06 — Principles

Restraint makes the system recognizable.

Every choice reinforces legibility, trust, and consistency across chains.

Do

  • Use blue for actions and selected states.
  • Use mono for hashes, addresses, and tickers.
  • Let hairlines create depth in both themes.
  • Keep financial content dense and scannable.

Do not

  • Use gradients, glows, or decorative blue.
  • Introduce photography or illustration.
  • Hide critical fees or transaction context.
  • Use shadows in any theme or component.
